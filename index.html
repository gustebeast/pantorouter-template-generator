Pantorouter Template Generator

Generates a parametric mortise/tenon template + matching rail for The Pantorouter, based on "Pantorouter Tenon Template" by FozzTexx (CC0). The rail prints separately to avoid needing structural support during printing.

Units
Tenon / Mortise

Dimensions of the joint that will be cut in the wood — not the size of the template itself.

Cutter
Mounting Spread the side screws perpendicular to the long axis so the template engages all three of the pantorouter's T-tracks (20 mm apart) instead of just one. Adds stability for tall narrow templates. Only available when the short-axis width is wide enough to fit the side countersinks with edge clearance.
Tenon fit-tuning Adds a 3 mm linear taper to the body's outer perimeter (wider at the bottom, narrower at the top). Lets you fine-tune tenon thickness by sliding the bearing along the taper. The dimensions you entered above land 2/3 of the way from the wide end to the narrow end, biasing most of the adjustment range toward "make tenon bigger".
