Pantorouter Template Generator

Generates a parametric mortise/tenon template + matching rail for The Pantorouter, based on FozzTexx's CC0 design. Everything runs in your browser — no install, no upload.

Click any file to download. STEP files open in any CAD viewer (Fusion 360, FreeCAD, OnShape, etc.). For 3D printing, send the STEP file to your slicer or convert to STL.

    What gets generated?
    template.step
    The main piece — routes the mortise and tenon. Has the dovetail slot in its back for the rail.
    rail.step
    Slides into the back of the template. Engages the pantorouter's T-track.
    assembly.step
    Both pieces in place — for visual verification only, not for printing.
    test_mortise.step / test_tenon.step
    Small test pieces (~20 mm) — print these first to verify the joint clearance before committing to the full template print.
    flat_mortise.step / flat_tenon.step
    2 mm-thick cross-section wafers. Print both, lay them flat side-by-side, and you can directly measure the slot/rail gap with calipers.
